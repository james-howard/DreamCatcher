Ignoring Errors

While spell checking using DreamCatcher you may find that there are several commonly used words that are marked as incorrect although they are simply not recognized by the dictionary. In this case, you can either add the word permanently to DreamCatcher's dictionary (refer to Modifying the Dictionary) or just ignore the word.
To ignore a word, right-click the misspelled word in the DreamCatcher results list and select one of the following:
  • Ignore: to ignore that particular instance of the word.
  • Ignore All: to ignore all instances in the Web site of the word.
