Modifying the Dictionary

You can add words to DreamCatcher's dictionary by selecting the word and selecting Add from the Spelling menu (or through the context menu). To view a list of words you have added to the dictionary, select Preferences from the DreamCatcher menu. When you add a word to the dictionary it will remain in the dictionary every time you use DreamCatcher. You can also remove words that you have added to DreamCatcher's dictionary through the preferences.
